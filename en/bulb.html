BULB

BULB is a concentrated series in which the light bulb remains deliberately visible. It appears like the fruit of a tree — fragile, present and unmistakably technical.

The wood does not act as a support but as a stage. The exposed light source defines the character of each object and turns the lamp into a clear, reduced statement.
