CLARITY

CLARITY is a lighter, more open series. Precisely worked wood meets a reduced construction and calm proportions. Mass is deliberately reduced in favor of structure, balance and transparency.

The objects appear restrained and architectural. Form and material remain clearly legible, without visual weight or ornament.

Wood · Concrete · Metal

Types: Floor lamps, table lamps

Materials: Wood, concrete, metal

Electrical: CE compliant · DIN-VDE certified
