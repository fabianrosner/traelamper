HALO

HALO is a series in which glass plays a central role. Glass vessels are used as lampshades, enveloping the light and creating a soft, atmospheric glow with strong spatial presence.

The combination of glass, wood and light creates a quiet tension between transparency and mass. The light is not directed but filtered and diffused into the surrounding space.

Glass · Wood · Light

Types: Table lamps, object lights

Materials: Glass, wood, metal

Electrical: CE compliant · DIN-VDE certified
