PURE

PURE is the most reduced series by Trae Lamper. Wood stands at the absolute center of attention. Light is used solely to reveal structure, surface and character.

There are no bases, no shades and no decorative elements. The technology recedes entirely, leaving only material and light.

Types: Table lamps, object lights
