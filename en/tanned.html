TANNED

TANNED is a series of sculptural lighting objects with pronounced physical presence. Strongly aged wood – especially oak – is combined with regional Grauwacke stone and blackened metal. Each piece develops directly from the material itself.

Wood · Stone · Metal

Types: Floor lamps, table lamps

Materials: Aged wood (incl. oak), Grauwacke, metal

Electrical: CE compliant · DIN-VDE certified

Dimensions & pricing: On request
