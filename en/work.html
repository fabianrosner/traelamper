Work

The works of Trae Lamper move between functional lighting and sculptural objects. Each series develops from the material itself and is conceived as an evolving body of work.

TANNED

Aged wood, stone and metal. Lighting objects with strong physical presence.

CLARITY

More open, lighter works. Reduced construction and clear proportions.

PURE

Wood as the central element. No base, no shade – only material and light.

BULB

The light bulb as a visible element. A strong, reduced statement.

HALO

Glass vessels as shades. Light is diffused and atmospherically filtered.
